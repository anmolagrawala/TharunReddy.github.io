Grants

The department provides you with the office room, some furniture and some equipment (a computer, a printer, etc., depending on the funds available – talk to the Head EE).

 

Apply for grants as soon as possible.

 

·       Institute Initiation Grant: info on DoRD website (no deadline) https://web.iitk.ac.in/july14dordn/initiation-grant

o   Use this money to get equipment, lab utilities, etc.

·       Startup Research Grant from SERB: (deadline: generally, in March) https://www.serbonline.in/SERB/srg_Instructions?HomePage=New

 

To get information about grants, talk to a person in the DoRD office (Saif currently (2021)). DoRD maintains a manual too:  https://web.iitk.ac.in/july14dordn/r-d-manual

Teaching

The normal teaching load per semester is 1.5 courses – the Head EE allocates that.

New Course

If you wish (you are highly encouraged) to float a new course, talk to the Head EE and the DPGC. For the first time, the course is floated as an EE698xx course. You just have to submit a course outline to the DPGC. After 1-2 times teaching, you can convert it into a regular course – the DPGC will circulate your course outline to faculty@iitk who can give their feedback; then, the course can be regularized by the DPGC.

 

Online Resources for Teaching

IITK has developed in-house the MooKIT platform as an LMS (learning management system). It is available at hello.iitk.ac.in.

 

Other online resources are:

·       MS-Teams: available for free through Office 365 account

·       Github classroom: github gives free pro account to educators. This is useful for coding based teaching/research.
